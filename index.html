Community Rules

A few simple rules.
One culture of rigour.

The quality of practice is the community's greatest asset. These rules protect it — non-negotiable for all members. Note that only answer keys are shared after each session; there are no solution discussions or worked explanations.

01
The 2-Hour Rule

Never post the final answer before the window closes. Working and reasoning only. First violation: warning. Second: removal.

02
Attempt Before You Discuss

Genuinely attempt every question before reading others' posts. Independence first, always.

03
Show Your Working

Post full working — not just "answer is (c)." A wrong approach with full reasoning beats a correct bare answer.

04
Respect All Levels

No mocking. No dismissing simpler questions. Every question was hard for someone once. Zero tolerance.

05
Post in the Right Group

Morning → Morning Session. Afternoon → Afternoon. Evening → Evening. Off-topic posts will be deleted.

06
No Spam or Forwarding

Do not forward questions or answers outside the community. No unrelated forwards inside either.
